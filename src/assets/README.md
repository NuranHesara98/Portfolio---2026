# Portfolio Assets

## Directory Structure

### /assets
- **profile.jpg** - Your profile photo for the hero section
- **/projects** - Screenshots/images of your projects
  - ecommerce.jpg
  - taskmanager.jpg
  - weather.jpg
  - social.jpg
  - portfolio.jpg
  - fitness.jpg
- **/certificates** - Images of your certificates
  - aws.jpg
  - gcp.jpg
  - mongodb.jpg
  - meta.jpg
  - kubernetes.jpg
- **/blogs** - Featured images for your blog posts
  - angular-scalable.jpg
  - microservices.jpg
  - css-layout.jpg
  - docker.jpg
  - typescript.jpg
  - rest-api.jpg

## Image Guidelines

### Profile Photo
- **Size**: 400x400px (square)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Quality**: High resolution

### Project Images
- **Size**: 800x600px (4:3 ratio)
- **Format**: JPG
- **Quality**: Web-optimized

### Certificate Images
- **Size**: 800x600px
- **Format**: JPG
- **Include**: Full certificate scan or screenshot

### Blog Images
- **Size**: 1200x630px (Featured image ratio)
- **Format**: JPG
- **Quality**: Web-optimized

## How to Add Your Images

1. Replace placeholder images with your actual images
2. Ensure filenames match those referenced in the components
3. Optimize images for web (compress without losing quality)
4. Use tools like TinyPNG or ImageOptim for compression

## Placeholder Images

Until you add your own images, you can use placeholder services:
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/) - Free high-quality photos
- [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/) - Free stock photos
- [Lorem Picsum](https://picsum.photos/) - Placeholder images
